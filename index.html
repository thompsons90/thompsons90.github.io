About

I am primarily a Front-end Developer based east of Dallas, TX. I thoroughly enjoy building both solo and team projects. I regulary connect and build with others in the FreeCodeCamp and React Dallas communities. I have spent years in the classroom as a Band Director, giving me a plethora of skills that I bring to the table as a developer, including: adaptability, ambition, communication, and punctuality.

In addition to these skills, I have a passion to grow and learn, improving my craft. Beyond coding, I enjoy time with my beautiful wife and two sweet kids, camping, sports, hunting, gaming, and church events.
